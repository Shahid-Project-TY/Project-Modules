Health Issues

Micro‑plastic Ingestion

Paper cups often contain a thin plastic lining that breaks down into micro‑plastics.

Chemical Leaching

Heat can cause harmful chemicals (e.g., BPA) to migrate into hot drinks.

Environmental Impact

Deforestation

Millions of trees are cut each year to produce disposable cups.

Water Pollution

Most paper cups end up in oceans, adding to plastic waste.
