Say No to Paper Cups

Protect your health, protect the Earth.

Health issues

Paper cups often have plastic linings and chemical additives that can leach into hot beverages, leading to potential health risks.

Microplastic ingestion

Hot liquids can release microplastics from cup linings, which may end up in your drink. Repeated exposure increases ingestion over time.

Chemical leaching

Adhesives, inks, and plasticizers can migrate into beverages, especially when exposed to heat, acidity, or long contact time.

Heat accelerates breakdown

High temperatures can accelerate the breakdown of coatings, increasing the release of compounds into hot drinks like tea or coffee.
