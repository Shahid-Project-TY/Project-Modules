Say No to Paper Cups

Protect your health, protect the Earth.

Health Issues

Microplastic Ingestion

Hot drinks in paper cups can leach tiny microplastics into your beverage, which may cause oxidative stress and organ issues when consumed frequently.

Chemical Leaching

Hazardous chemicals like phthalates, lead, chromium, and endocrine disruptors can leach from the plastic lining into hot beverages, raising the risk of hormonal imbalance and cancer.

Chronic Health Risks

Regular exposure increases risk of cancer, hormonal disruption, and immune system problems due to persistent intake of toxins.

Environmental Impact

  • Deforestation: Billions of trees are cut yearly for paper cups, reducing biodiversity.
  • Waste Pollution: Paper cups, with their plastic linings, aren't recyclable in most facilities, piling up in landfills and water bodies.
  • Water Contamination: Chemicals and microplastics leach into waterways, harming aquatic life.
